How to make omellete

Today's recipes is going to be easy and simple. we are just going to make a omellete which is a great fast and nutrient rich meal

So without any further ado let's begin

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • parsely
  • spices

Steps:

  1. Chop up your parsely
  2. crack your egg in a bowl and mix in the spices with parsely
  3. finally cook the egg in a frying pan
  4. Now enjoy!
